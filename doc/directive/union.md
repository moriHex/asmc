## UNION

_name_ **UNION** [[alignment]] [[, NONUNIQUE]]

fielddeclarations

[[_name_]] ENDS

Declares a union of one or more data types. The fielddeclarations must be valid data definitions. Omit the ENDS name label on nested UNION definitions.
